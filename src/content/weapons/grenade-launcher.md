## Overview

The **Grenade Launcher** is a heavy weapon in GTA 6, modeled after the Milkor MGL or M203. It features a 6-round revolving cylinder that can fire grenades in quick succession with arcing trajectories to hit targets behind cover.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Revolving Grenade Launcher
- **Real-World Counterpart:** Milkor MGL / M203
- **Cylinder Capacity:** 6 rounds
- **Damage:** High (multiple rounds)
- **Effective Range:** 100–150 meters
- **Blast Radius:** ~15 meters per grenade

## Customization

- Sights (iron sights, red dot, 3x scope, laser)
- Extended 8-round cylinder
- Grenade types (fragmentation, armor-piercing, smoke, incendiary)
- Folding stock
- Different grips

## How to Obtain

- Purchase from Ammu-Nation
- Special locations
- Mission rewards
