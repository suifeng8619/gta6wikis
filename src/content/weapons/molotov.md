## Overview

The **Molotov Cocktail** is an incendiary throwable weapon in GTA 6. It ignites instantly on impact, creating a fire zone of approximately 5 meters that burns for 10–15 seconds, dealing continuous damage.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Incendiary Throwable
- **Real-World Counterpart:** Molotov cocktail (improvised incendiary)
- **Damage:** Medium (continuous burn damage)
- **Ignition:** Instant on impact
- **Fire Radius:** ~5 meters (spreading flames)
- **Burn Duration:** 10–15 seconds
- **Carry Limit:** ~8–12

## How to Obtain

- Purchase from Ammu-Nation
- Special locations
- Enemy drops
