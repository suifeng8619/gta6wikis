## Overview

The **Pistol** is the standard semi-automatic handgun in GTA 6, modeled after the Taurus PT92AF / Beretta M9. It serves as a reliable starting sidearm with balanced damage, standard fire rate, and standard accuracy.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Handgun
- **Real-World Counterpart:** Taurus PT92AF / Beretta M9
- **Magazine Capacity:** ~17 rounds
- **Damage:** Medium
- **Fire Rate:** Standard
- **Accuracy:** Standard

## Customization

The Pistol supports the following attachments:

- Suppressor (reduces detection range)
- Scope / red dot sight
- Different ammo types (standard, armor-piercing, incendiary)

## How to Obtain

- Purchase from Ammu-Nation
- Loot from defeated enemies
- Found on the ground during missions
