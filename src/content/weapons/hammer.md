## Overview

The **Hammer** is a heavy-hitting blunt melee weapon in GTA 6, modeled after a standard claw hammer. It delivers medium-high damage with a slower attack speed and can be used to break objects and smash doors.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Blunt Weapon (Hammer)
- **Real-World Counterpart:** Standard claw hammer
- **Damage:** Medium-high (heavy strikes)
- **Attack Speed:** Medium to slow
- **Range:** Short
- **Special:** Can break objects and doors

## How to Obtain

- Found in tool rooms
- Construction sites
- Garages
