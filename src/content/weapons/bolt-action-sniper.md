## Overview

The **Bolt Action Sniper** is a precision bolt-action sniper rifle in GTA 6, modeled after the Remington 700 / M700 series. It delivers extreme damage per shot with extreme accuracy, capable of one-shot eliminations on most targets.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Bolt Action Sniper Rifle
- **Real-World Counterpart:** Remington 700 / M700 series
- **Magazine Capacity:** 5 rounds
- **Damage:** Extremely high (one-shot kill on most targets)
- **Fire Rate:** Low (manual bolt action)
- **Accuracy:** Extremely high

## Customization

- High-power scopes (8x, 10x, 12x, variable 4–12x)
- Suppressor
- Different stocks

## How to Obtain

- Purchase from Ammu-Nation
- Special missions
- Premium dealers
