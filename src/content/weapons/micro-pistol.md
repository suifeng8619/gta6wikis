## Overview

The **Micro Pistol** is a compact concealed-carry handgun in GTA 6, modeled after the Bersa Firestorm .380. The in-game name may be "Bantam" or "Micro Pistol" (not yet confirmed). It offers low-to-medium damage with a fast fire rate.

- **Real-World Counterpart:** Bersa Firestorm .380
