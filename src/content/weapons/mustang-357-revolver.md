## Overview

The **Mustang .357 Revolver** is a powerful revolver in GTA 6, modeled after the Colt Python .357. It features very high damage and high accuracy but suffers from a slow fire rate due to the revolver mechanism.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Revolver
- **Real-World Counterpart:** Colt Python .357
- **Cylinder Capacity:** 6 rounds
- **Damage:** Very high
- **Fire Rate:** Low (revolver reload speed)
- **Accuracy:** High

## Customization

- Different barrel lengths
- Scope
- Wooden grip variants

## How to Obtain

- Purchase from Ammu-Nation
- Special missions
- Rare drops
