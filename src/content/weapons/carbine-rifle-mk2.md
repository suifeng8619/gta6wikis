## Overview

The **Carbine Rifle Mk II** is an improved variant of the Carbine Rifle in GTA 6, based on an upgraded AR-15 platform. It features expanded customization options with advanced optics, premium suppressors, and special ammunition types.

- **Type:** Improved Carbine Rifle
- **Real-World Counterpart:** AR-15 platform (improved variant)
- **Magazine Capacity:** 30 rounds
- **Damage:** High
- **Fire Rate:** Fast (possibly adjustable)
- **Accuracy:** High

## Customization

- Premium suppressor
- Different ammo types (armor-piercing, incendiary)
