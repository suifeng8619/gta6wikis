## Overview

The **Heavy Pistol** is a high-damage handgun in GTA 6, modeled after the iconic M1911 pistol. It trades magazine capacity for superior stopping power per shot.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Handgun
- **Real-World Counterpart:** M1911
- **Magazine Capacity:** ~7 rounds
- **Damage:** High
- **Fire Rate:** Standard
- **Accuracy:** Standard
