## Overview

The **Crowbar** is a versatile melee weapon in GTA 6 that doubles as a tool. It delivers medium-high damage at medium attack speed and can be used to pry open doors and locks.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Bladed Blunt Weapon
- **Real-World Counterpart:** Standard crowbar / pry bar
- **Damage:** Medium-high
- **Attack Speed:** Medium
- **Range:** Short
- **Special:** Can pry open doors and locks

## How to Obtain

- Found in tool rooms
- Construction sites
- On the ground
