## Overview

The **Double Barrel Shotgun** is a devastating close-range weapon in GTA 6, modeled after a classic double-barrel hunting shotgun. It fires two shells with extreme damage but must be reloaded after each pair of shots.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Double Barrel Shotgun
- **Real-World Counterpart:** Double-barrel hunting shotgun (Over/Under or Side-by-Side)
- **Capacity:** 2 rounds
- **Damage:** Extremely high
- **Fire Rate:** Low (requires reload after 2 shots)
- **Accuracy:** Low (wide pellet spread)

## Customization

- Laser sight
- Different barrel configurations (side-by-side, over/under)
- Different barrel lengths

## How to Obtain

- Purchase from Ammu-Nation
- Special locations
