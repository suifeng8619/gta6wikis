## Overview

The **SMG** is the standard submachine gun in GTA 6, modeled after the H&K MP5 or a similar European submachine gun. It delivers low-to-medium damage with a very fast fire rate and medium accuracy.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Submachine Gun
- **Real-World Counterpart:** H&K MP5 or similar
- **Magazine Capacity:** 30 rounds
- **Damage:** Low to medium
- **Fire Rate:** Very fast
- **Accuracy:** Medium
