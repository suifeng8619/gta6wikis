## Overview

The **Hunter Sniper** is a professional-grade bolt-action sniper rifle in GTA 6, modeled after the Arctic Warfare L96A1 / Accuracy International AWS. It provides extreme damage and extreme accuracy, optimized for long-distance precision shots.

## Specifications

- **Type:** High-Precision Bolt Action Sniper Rifle
- **Real-World Counterpart:** Arctic Warfare L96A1 / Accuracy International AWS
- **Magazine Capacity:** 5 rounds
- **Damage:** Extremely high
- **Fire Rate:** Low (bolt action)
- **Accuracy:** Extremely high (professional-grade)

## Customization

- Advanced optics (10x, 12x, 15x ultra-range scope)
- Variable power scope
- Suppressor
- Heavy stock

## How to Obtain

- Purchase from Ammu-Nation (unlock required)
- Special missions
