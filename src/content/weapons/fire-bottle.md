## Overview

The **Fire Bottle** is an improved incendiary throwable weapon in GTA 6. It creates a slightly larger burn area (~5–6 meters) with a longer burn duration (12–18 seconds) than the standard Molotov Cocktail.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Incendiary Throwable
- **Real-World Counterpart:** Improved incendiary bottle design
- **Damage:** Medium (high-temperature burn)
- **Ignition:** Instant on impact
- **Fire Radius:** ~5–6 meters
- **Burn Duration:** 12–18 seconds
- **Carry Limit:** ~8–12

## How to Obtain

- Purchase from Ammu-Nation
- Found near restaurants or bars
- Mission rewards
