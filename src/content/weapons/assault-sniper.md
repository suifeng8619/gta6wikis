## Overview

The **Assault Sniper** is a semi-automatic sniper rifle in GTA 6, modeled after the L129A1 Sharpshooter Rifle. It enables faster follow-up shots compared to bolt-action snipers, with a larger magazine capacity suited for engaging multiple targets.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Semi-Automatic Sniper Rifle
- **Real-World Counterpart:** L129A1 Sharpshooter Rifle
- **Magazine Capacity:** 10–20 rounds
- **Damage:** High to extremely high
- **Fire Rate:** Faster than bolt-action, supports rapid follow-up
- **Accuracy:** Very high

## Customization

- Variable power scopes (4x, 8x, 3.5–10x)
- Suppressor
- Tactical grip
- Different magazines

## How to Obtain

- Purchase from Ammu-Nation
- Mission rewards
