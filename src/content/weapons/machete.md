## Overview

The **Machete** is a large blade melee weapon in GTA 6, modeled after a real machete / cutlass. It delivers high damage with medium attack speed and offers greater reach than smaller knives. It can also cut through brush and vegetation.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Large Blade
- **Real-World Counterpart:** Large machete / cutlass
- **Damage:** High
- **Attack Speed:** Medium (swing motion)
- **Range:** Medium (longer blade)
- **Special:** Can cut through vegetation

## How to Obtain

- Found in jungle areas
- Tool shops
- Enemy drops
