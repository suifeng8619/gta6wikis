## Overview

The **Speargun** is a specialized underwater weapon in GTA 6, modeled after a real underwater speargun / harpoon gun. It fires projectiles with high penetrating damage at approximately 15-meter effective range, primarily designed for aquatic missions.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Underwater Projectile Weapon
- **Real-World Counterpart:** Underwater speargun / harpoon gun
- **Damage:** High (penetrating)
- **Fire Delay:** None (instant projectile)
- **Effective Range:** ~15 meters
- **Carry Limit:** ~6–10 spears

## How to Obtain

- Found at underwater locations
- Diving missions
- Special equipment vendors
