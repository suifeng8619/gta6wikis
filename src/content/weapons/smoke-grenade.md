## Overview

The **Smoke Grenade** is a tactical throwable in GTA 6, modeled after the M18 smoke grenade. It creates a smoke screen that blocks line of sight within an approximately 8-meter radius for 5–10 seconds.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Smoke Screen Device
- **Real-World Counterpart:** M18 Smoke Grenade
- **Damage:** None
- **Smoke Effect:** Blocks line of sight for 5–10 seconds
- **Smoke Radius:** ~8 meters
- **Carry Limit:** ~8–12

## How to Obtain

- Purchase from Ammu-Nation
- Military missions
- Special locations
