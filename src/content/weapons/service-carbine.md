## Overview

The **Service Carbine** is a military-grade rifle in GTA 6, modeled after the M16A4 or M4A1. It provides high damage and high accuracy, making it a top-tier assault rifle for tactical engagements.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Military Carbine
- **Real-World Counterpart:** M16A4 / M4A1
- **Magazine Capacity:** 30 rounds
- **Damage:** High
- **Fire Rate:** Standard to fast
- **Accuracy:** High

## Customization

- Extended magazine
- Tactical grip
- Different stocks
