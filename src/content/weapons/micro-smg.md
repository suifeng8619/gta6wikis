## Overview

The **Micro SMG** is a micro submachine gun in GTA 6, modeled after the Mini Uzi or Tec-9. It has an extremely fast fire rate with the possibility of dual-wielding, but low accuracy.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Micro Submachine Gun
- **Real-World Counterpart:** Mini Uzi / Tec-9
- **Magazine Capacity:** 20–25 rounds
- **Damage:** Low
- **Fire Rate:** Extremely fast (dual-wield possible)
- **Accuracy:** Low
