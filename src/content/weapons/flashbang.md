## Overview

The **Flashbang** is a non-lethal stun grenade in GTA 6, modeled after the M84 stun grenade. It produces a blinding flash and loud detonation that disables enemy vision for 3–5 seconds within an approximately 6-meter radius.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Stun Grenade
- **Real-World Counterpart:** M84 Stun Grenade
- **Damage:** None (non-lethal)
- **Flash Effect:** Blinds enemies for 3–5 seconds
- **Sound Effect:** High-decibel detonation
- **Effect Radius:** ~6 meters
- **Carry Limit:** ~8–12

## How to Obtain

- Purchase from Ammu-Nation
- SWAT equipment
- Special missions
