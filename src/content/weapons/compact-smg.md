## Overview

The **Compact SMG** is a compact submachine gun in GTA 6, modeled after the Scorpion EVO 3 or IWI Uzi Pro. It features an extremely fast fire rate but low accuracy, designed for rapid close-range exchanges.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Compact Submachine Gun
- **Real-World Counterpart:** Scorpion EVO 3 / IWI Uzi Pro
- **Magazine Capacity:** 25–30 rounds
- **Damage:** Low
- **Fire Rate:** Extremely fast
- **Accuracy:** Low
