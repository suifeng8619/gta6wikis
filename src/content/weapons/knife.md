## Overview

The **Knife** is a fast melee weapon in GTA 6, modeled after a multi-purpose tactical knife. It delivers high damage with quick attack speed at very close range, making it ideal for silent stealth eliminations.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Blade Weapon
- **Real-World Counterpart:** Multi-purpose tactical knife
- **Damage:** High (rapid stabs)
- **Attack Speed:** Fast
- **Range:** Very short (close contact)
- **Special:** Silent elimination, stealth damage multiplier

## How to Obtain

- Found in kitchens
- Knife shops
- Enemy drops
