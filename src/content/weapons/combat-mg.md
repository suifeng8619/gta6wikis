## Overview

The **Combat MG** is a light machine gun in GTA 6, modeled after the M249 SAW (Squad Automatic Weapon) / FN Minimi. It features a massive 200-round belt-fed capacity for sustained suppressive fire, though it is heavy and reduces movement speed.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Light Machine Gun
- **Real-World Counterpart:** M249 SAW / FN Minimi
- **Belt Capacity:** 200 rounds
- **Damage:** Medium to high
- **Fire Rate:** Fast to very fast
- **Accuracy:** Medium (degrades during sustained fire)

## Customization

- Suppressor
- Extended belt (300 rounds)
- Various optics (red dot, holographic, 4x scope)
- Bipod
- Tactical stock
- Different grips

## How to Obtain

- Purchase from Ammu-Nation (premium)
- Mission rewards
- Special locations (military bases)
