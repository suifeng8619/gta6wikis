## Overview

The **RPG** (Rocket Launcher) is a heavy weapon in GTA 6, modeled after the RPG-7 or M202 FLASH. It fires rockets with extreme explosive damage and an approximately 20-meter blast radius, capable of destroying vehicles and eliminating groups of enemies in a single shot.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Rocket Launcher
- **Real-World Counterpart:** RPG-7 / M202 FLASH
- **Capacity:** 5 rockets
- **Damage:** Extremely high (wide-area explosion)
- **Effective Range:** 150–200 meters
- **Blast Radius:** ~20 meters

## Customization

- Different sights (red dot, 4x scope, thermal imaging)
- Rocket types (high-explosive, armor-piercing, incendiary)
- Enhanced capacity (7–10 rockets)

## How to Obtain

- Purchase from Ammu-Nation (unlock required)
- Military locations
- Special missions
