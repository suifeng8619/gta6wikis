## Overview

The **Carbine Rifle** is a versatile assault rifle in GTA 6, modeled after the AR-15/M4 Carbine. It offers higher accuracy than the standard Assault Rifle with comparable damage and fire rate.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Carbine Rifle
- **Real-World Counterpart:** AR-15 / M4 Carbine
- **Magazine Capacity:** 30 rounds
- **Damage:** High
- **Fire Rate:** Fast
- **Accuracy:** High

## Customization

- Multiple scope options
- Suppressor
- Grip variants (tactical, vertical, angled)
- Different stocks
- Laser designator

## How to Obtain

- Purchase from Ammu-Nation
- Mission rewards
