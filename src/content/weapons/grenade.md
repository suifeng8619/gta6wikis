## Overview

The **Grenade** is a standard explosive throwable weapon in GTA 6, modeled after the M67 hand grenade. It features a 3–4 second fuse delay and an approximately 8-meter blast radius.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Explosive Hand Grenade
- **Real-World Counterpart:** M67 Hand Grenade
- **Damage:** High (within blast radius)
- **Fuse Delay:** 3–4 seconds
- **Blast Radius:** ~8 meters
- **Carry Limit:** ~10–15

## How to Obtain

- Purchase from Ammu-Nation
- Enemy drops
- Mission rewards
- Found on the ground
