## Overview

The **Pump Action Shotgun** is a powerful close-range weapon in GTA 6, modeled after the Mossberg 590 or Remington 870. It delivers extreme damage at close range but requires manual pump action between shots.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Pump Action Shotgun
- **Real-World Counterpart:** Mossberg 590 / Remington 870
- **Magazine Capacity:** 8 rounds (expandable)
- **Damage:** Extremely high (close range)
- **Fire Rate:** Low (pump action)
- **Accuracy:** Low (pellet spread)

## Customization

- Extended magazine (10–12 rounds)
- Various barrel lengths (short, standard, long)
- Red dot sight / holographic sight / laser
- Different stocks and grips
- Suppressor

## How to Obtain

- Purchase from Ammu-Nation
- Mission rewards
