## Overview

The **Pool Cue** is a long-reach melee weapon in GTA 6, modeled after a billiard / pool cue stick. It delivers medium damage with fast jabbing attacks and offers medium-to-long reach.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Long Blunt Weapon
- **Real-World Counterpart:** Billiard / pool cue stick
- **Damage:** Medium
- **Attack Speed:** Fast (jabbing motion)
- **Range:** Medium to long
- **Special:** Silent attack, long-range jab

## How to Obtain

- Found in pool halls
- Bars
- On the ground
