## Overview

The **Assault Rifle** is the standard automatic rifle in GTA 6, modeled after the AK-47 or AK-74. It delivers high damage with a fast fire rate, though accuracy drops at longer distances.

## Specifications

- **Type:** Assault Rifle
- **Real-World Counterpart:** AK-47 / AK-74
- **Magazine Capacity:** 30 rounds (expandable to 40+)
- **Damage:** High
- **Fire Rate:** Fast
- **Accuracy:** Medium
