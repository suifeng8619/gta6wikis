## Character Description

Lori Heder is Brian Heder's third wife, working alongside him at their boat yard in the Leonida Keys. She is described as being "comfortable with weapons," handling both assault rifles and pistols with proficiency.

Beyond her role as Brian's wife, Lori is an active participant in the boat yard operations—both legitimate and criminal. She is known for making sangria, which is mentioned in the context of Jason's living arrangement (Brian expects Jason to join for Lori's sangria).

## Relationship to Brian Heder

Lori is Brian's third wife, suggesting Brian has a complicated marital history. Their partnership extends from personal life into criminal operations, with Lori serving as both business partner and spouse at the Leonida Keys boat yard.
