## Character Description

Dre'Quan Priest is described as "always more of a hustler than a gangster." He started as a street-level drug dealer but broke into the music industry to escape street life despite needing the income. His hustle mentality carried over from the streets into the entertainment business.

Now he is "setting his sights on the Vice City scene" for major growth, seeking the breakthrough hit record that will establish his label's credibility.

## Background

Dre'Quan's journey represents a transition from street crime to entrepreneurship:
- **Origin**: Street-level drug dealer
- **Motivation**: Music was always the goal despite needing street income
- **Transition**: Broke into music industry to escape street life
- **Current Status**: Young aspiring music mogul building his empire

## Business Operations

### Only Raw Records

Dre'Quan founded and manages Only Raw Records, his music label:

- **Primary Act**: Real Dimez (Bae-Luxe and Roxy) as the label's signed artists
- **Business Model**: Seeking breakthrough hit to establish label credibility
- **Growth Strategy**: Booking acts into Boobie Ike's strip club while building the label
- **Productions**: Music production and distribution
- **Marketing**: Social media presence and artist development

### Partnership with Boobie Ike

Dre'Quan partnered with Boobie Ike, who invested in Only Raw Records:
- Boobie's Jack of Hearts strip club serves as a performance venue
- Financial backing from Boobie's business empire
- Shared interest in music industry legitimacy
- Combined network of entertainment and criminal connections

- **Risk Profile**: Moderate; balancing street connections with business legitimacy
