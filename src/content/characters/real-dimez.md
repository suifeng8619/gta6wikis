## Collective Profile

The Real Dimez are a female hip-hop rap duo consisting of Bae-Luxe and Roxy, friends since high school. They are described as "savvy to turn their time shaking down local dealers into cold, hard cash via spicy rap tracks" and maintaining a "relentless social media presence."

After five years of hustle, they signed to Dre'Quan Priest's Only Raw Records label, hoping that "lightning can strike twice" following their earlier success.

## Career Trajectory

- **Origins**: High school friends who transitioned from street hustling
- **Initial Success**: Early hit single "DWNPLY" with local rapper DWNPLY
- **Peak Fame**: Major social media following during initial success
- **Current Status**: Relative one-hit wonder seeking comeback
- **Label**: Signed to Only Raw Records after five years
- **Aspiration**: New breakthrough hit to recapture earlier success

## Business Model

The Real Dimez operate at the intersection of music and social media:
- **Income Source**: Initially monetized street credibility through rap tracks
- **Transition**: Evolved from street hustling to music industry as primary income
- **Social Media Strategy**: "Relentless social media presence" drives audience growth
- **Monetization**: Music sales, streaming, social media deals
