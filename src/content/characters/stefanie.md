## Character Description

Stefanie is a correctional social worker employed by the Leonida Department of Corrections, stationed at Leonida Penitentiary in Vice-Dale County. She was assigned to Lucia Caminos during Lucia's imprisonment, serving as her counselor and rehabilitation contact.

Stefanie is responsible for the most iconic line in GTA 6 marketing: "Lucia, do you know why you're here?"—the first dialogue spoken in the franchise's history-making first trailer.

## Professional Role

As a correctional social worker, Stefanie's duties include:
- **Inmate Evaluation**: Assessing prisoner rehabilitation potential
- **Counseling**: Addressing behavioral and psychological issues
- **Reintegration Planning**: Preparing inmates for post-release transition
- **Relationship Building**: Establishing trust with inmates
- **Documentation**: Maintaining records of inmate progress

## Connection to Lucia Caminos

Stefanie served as Lucia's correctional officer during her imprisonment at Leonida Penitentiary. The prison interview scene in Trailer 1 establishes the dynamic between the two characters—Stefanie as the authority figure asking probing questions, Lucia as the defiant inmate responding with her signature cynicism.

## Trailer Appearance

### Trailer 1 (December 2023)

Stefanie appears in the opening scene of GTA 6's first trailer. The scene takes place in a prison interview room at Leonida Penitentiary where Stefanie asks Lucia the now-famous question: "Lucia, do you know why you're here?" This opening establishes the game's grounded, character-driven tone.
