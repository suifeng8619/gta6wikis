## Character Description

Cal Hampton is Jason Duval's close friend and fellow associate of Brian Heder. Officially described as someone who "feels safest hanging at home, snooping on Coast Guard comms with a few beers and some private browser tabs open," Cal is a tech-savvy loner living in digital shadows.

Cal is convinced that every internet rumor has a grain of truth, reflecting a deeply paranoid but functional worldview. Despite his preference for staying off the radar, he maintains close relationships within Jason's circle.

## Technology Expertise

Cal possesses amateur signal intelligence capabilities, operating Coast Guard communications interception equipment from his home. His surveillance monitoring skills and knowledge of restricted radio channels make him a valuable intelligence asset within the criminal network.

Cal serves as Jason's close friend, contrasting with Jason's larger ambitions. While Cal prefers a low-profile lifestyle, Jason has "bigger plans" for Cal's specialized skills than Cal himself recognizes. This relationship suggests Jason acts as a catalyst, drawing seemingly unambitious individuals into criminal operations.
