## Character Description

Brian Heder is described as a "classic drug runner from the glory days of smuggling in the Keys." An old man with extensive criminal history, Brian still moves product through his boat yard while operating his legitimate business with his third wife, Lori.

Despite maintaining a low public profile, Brian is a ruthless smuggler who controls significant drug trafficking operations through the Leonida Keys waterways.

## Business Operations

Brian operates a boat yard in the Leonida Keys as both a legitimate business and a front for his smuggling network. The boat yard serves as the logistics hub for drug trafficking operations, with his wife Lori serving as both business partner and active participant in criminal activities.

## Relationship to Jason Duval

Brian serves multiple roles in Jason's life:

- **Landlord**: Allows Jason to live rent-free at one of his properties in Key Lento
- **Employer**: Requires Jason to help with local shakedowns and collection activities
- **Mentor**: Described as a "mentor or big-brother figure" to Jason
- **Connection**: Links Jason to the broader Leonida Keys criminal network

The conditions of Jason's free housing include helping with Brian's criminal operations and joining for Lori's sangria—suggesting a relationship that blends criminal hierarchy with familial familiarity.

## Criminal Network

Brian's smuggling network represents the established criminal infrastructure of the Leonida Keys. His operations include:
- Drug trafficking through maritime routes
- Boat yard as logistics and cover operation
- Shakedowns and intimidation through associates like Jason
- Connections to broader Vice City criminal enterprises

## Marriage to Lori Heder

Brian is on his third marriage, to Lori Heder. Lori works alongside him at the boat yard and is described as being "comfortable with weapons," handling both assault rifles and pistols. Their partnership extends from personal life into criminal operations.

## Trailer Appearance

Brian Heder is prominently featured in Trailer 2, establishing his role as a key figure in the Leonida Keys criminal ecosystem and his relationship with Jason Duval.
