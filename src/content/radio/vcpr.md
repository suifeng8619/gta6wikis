## Vice City Public Radio (VCPR)

VCPR is one of the nine radio stations in GTA Vice City (2002). Broadcasting on the AM band, it is a public radio station primarily featuring the talk show "Pressing Issues," hosted by Maurice Chavez (voiced by Philip Anthony-Rodriguez).

### Pressing Issues

The station's main program discusses social issues including morality, public safety, and positive thinking. The show is known for its satirical and confrontational discussion style, with host Maurice Chavez often becoming frustrated with his guests.

### Station Character

VCPR parodies National Public Radio and public broadcasting in general, providing sharp social commentary wrapped in humor. It serves as one of the key world-building elements of Vice City's media landscape.
