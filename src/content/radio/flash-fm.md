## Flash FM

Flash FM is one of the nine radio stations in GTA Vice City (2002). Hosted by DJ Toni, the station plays upbeat 80s mainstream pop music. With 17 tracks in its rotation, Flash FM captures the lively, carefree spirit of the 1980s pop scene.

### Station Character

Flash FM represents the commercial pop side of 1980s music culture. The station features some of the decade's most recognizable hits, making it a fan favorite for cruising through Vice City's neon-lit streets.

### Notable Tracks

The station's playlist includes iconic songs like A-ha's "Take on Me," Wham!'s "Wake Me Up Before You Go-Go," and Bonnie Tyler's "Total Eclipse of the Heart" -- all defining tracks of the 80s pop era.
