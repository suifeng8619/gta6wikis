## Soulwax FM

Soulwax FM is one of 26 radio stations in GTA V (2013) and GTA Online. Curated by the Belgian band Soulwax, the station features electronic and house music.

### Station Character

Soulwax FM brings a European electronic music perspective to Los Santos, with the band's signature blend of dance-floor-ready tracks curated with attention to quality and diversity within the electronic genre.
