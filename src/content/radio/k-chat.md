## K-Chat

K-Chat is one of the nine radio stations in GTA Vice City (2002). It is an interactive talk show station broadcasting on the AM band, featuring jokes, celebrity gossip, and entertainment discussions.

### Station Character

Unlike the music stations, K-Chat provides comedic relief and world-building through its talk show format. The station satirizes celebrity culture and entertainment media of the 1980s.
