## West Coast Classics

West Coast Classics is one of 26 radio stations in GTA V (2013) and GTA Online. The station plays classic 1990s West Coast hip-hop, celebrating the golden era of gangsta rap.

### Station Character

West Coast Classics pays tribute to the golden age of West Coast hip-hop, featuring iconic artists who defined the genre. The station provides a nostalgic complement to the modern hip-hop of Radio Los Santos.
