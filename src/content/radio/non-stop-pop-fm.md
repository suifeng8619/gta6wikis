## Non-Stop-Pop FM

Non-Stop-Pop FM is one of 26 radio stations in GTA V (2013) and GTA Online. The station plays mainstream pop music and dance tracks.

### Station Character

Non-Stop-Pop FM is the go-to station for commercial pop music in Los Santos, featuring chart-topping hits that represent the modern pop landscape. It serves as the spiritual successor to stations like Flash FM from GTA Vice City.
