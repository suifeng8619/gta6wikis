## East Los FM

East Los FM is one of 26 radio stations in GTA V (2013) and GTA Online. Hosted by Don Cheto and Camilo Lara, the station plays Latin American music including Spanish-language pop, reggaeton, and Mexican electronic music.

### Station Character

East Los FM represents Los Santos' vibrant Latin American community, bringing authentic Spanish-language music to the game. The dual hosting by Don Cheto and Camilo Lara provides cultural authenticity and personality to the station.
