## Emotion 98.3

Emotion 98.3 is one of the nine radio stations in GTA Vice City (2002). Hosted by Fernando Martinez, the station plays romantic 80s power ballads and soft rock. With 17 tracks, it provides the perfect soundtrack for Vice City's more sentimental moments.

### Station Character

The station specializes in love songs and emotional ballads from the 1980s. Fernando Martinez's hosting style adds a romantic and sometimes humorous personality to the station's atmosphere.

### Notable Tracks

The playlist includes timeless classics like Phil Collins' "In the Air Tonight," Foreigner's "I Want to Know What Love Is," and Luther Vandross' "Never Too Much."
