## Rebel Radio

Rebel Radio is one of 26 radio stations in GTA V (2013) and GTA Online. The station plays classic American country music.

### Station Character

Rebel Radio represents the rural side of the GTA V world, particularly fitting for the Blaine County countryside areas. The station brings traditional country music to the game, complementing Trevor's desert lifestyle and the game's rural exploration.
