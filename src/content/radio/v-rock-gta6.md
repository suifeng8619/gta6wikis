## V-Rock Returns to GTA 6

V-Rock is the only radio station officially confirmed to appear in GTA 6. The confirmation came from gameplay footage showing protagonist Jason wearing a V-Rock T-shirt, confirming the iconic rock station's return from GTA Vice City.

### Confirmed Details

- **Status**: Officially confirmed (from GTA Vice City return)
- **Confirmation Source**: Jason wearing V-Rock T-shirt in gameplay footage
- **Expected Genre**: Rock / Heavy Metal (continuing the original style)

### Legacy

V-Rock originally appeared in GTA Vice City (2002) where it was hosted by Lazlow and featured 80s hard rock and heavy metal music. The station became one of the most iconic radio stations in the GTA franchise.

### What to Expect

While specific song lists and DJ details for the GTA 6 version have not been officially revealed, the station is expected to continue its tradition of rock and metal music, potentially featuring both classic tracks and modern rock artists.
