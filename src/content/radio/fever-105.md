## Fever 105

Fever 105 is one of the nine radio stations in GTA Vice City (2002). Hosted by Oliver "Ladykiller" Biscuit (voiced by Julius Dyson), the station plays disco, soul, funk, and R&B from the 1970s and 1980s. The station features 17 tracks.

### Station Character

Fever 105 captures the dance music spirit of the late 70s and early 80s, with a focus on black music genres that defined the era. The station brings the energy of disco nights and funk-fueled dance floors to Vice City.

### Notable Tracks

The playlist includes classics like Michael Jackson's "Wanna Be Startin' Somethin'," Kool & The Gang's "Summer Madness," and Indeep's "Last Night a DJ Saved My Life."
