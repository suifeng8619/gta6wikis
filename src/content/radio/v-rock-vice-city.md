## V-Rock

V-Rock is one of the most iconic radio stations in GTA Vice City (2002). Hosted by Lazlow (voiced by Lazlow Jones), the station originally plays 17 tracks spanning hard rock, new wave, and electronic music from the late 1970s to mid-1980s.

### Station Character

Lazlow is a passionate rock enthusiast whose energetic hosting style and commentary between songs defined the station's personality. V-Rock became one of the most memorable radio stations in the entire GTA franchise.

### Notable Tracks

The station features legendary tracks including Gary Numan's "Cars," Blondie's "Atomic," Duran Duran's "Hungry Like the Wolf," and Tears for Fears' "Everybody Wants to Rule the World."

### Copyright Status

The original 2002 release contained all 17 songs. Post-2012 versions lost some tracks due to expired licenses. The Definitive Edition also had some songs removed.

### GTA 6 Legacy

V-Rock has been confirmed to return in GTA 6, spotted on protagonist Jason's T-shirt in gameplay footage -- the only radio station officially confirmed for the new game.
