## Radio Los Santos

Radio Los Santos is one of 26 radio stations in GTA V (2013) and GTA Online. It is the game's main hip-hop and rap station, featuring modern mainstream hip-hop music.

### Station Character

As the primary hip-hop station, Radio Los Santos reflects the contemporary rap scene of Los Santos, tying into the game's street culture themes, particularly Franklin's storyline.
