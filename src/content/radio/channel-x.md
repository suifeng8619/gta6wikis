## Channel X

Channel X is one of 26 radio stations in GTA V (2013) and GTA Online. The station plays punk rock and hardcore punk music, representing the underground and rebellious side of the Los Santos music scene.

### Station Character

Channel X embodies the anti-establishment spirit of punk rock, featuring raw, aggressive music that contrasts with the more polished sounds of other GTA V stations. It appeals to players seeking high-energy, rebellious soundtracks for their gameplay.
