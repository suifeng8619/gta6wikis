## Los Santos Rock Radio

Los Santos Rock Radio is one of 26 radio stations in GTA V (2013) and GTA Online. It serves as the game's primary rock station, playing traditional and contemporary rock music.

### Station Character

The station represents the mainstream rock listening experience in Los Santos, featuring a mix of classic rock staples and newer rock tracks that appeal to a broad audience.
