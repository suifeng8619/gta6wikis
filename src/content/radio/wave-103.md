## Wave 103

Wave 103 is one of the nine radio stations in GTA Vice City (2002). Hosted by Adam First (voiced by Jared Harris), the station plays new wave and synthpop music from the 1980s. With 18 tracks, it has the largest playlist among Vice City stations.

### Station Character

Wave 103 represents the British new wave and avant-garde music movement of the 1980s. Adam First's hosting brings an artistic, forward-thinking personality to the station.

### Copyright Significance

Wave 103 is the only GTA Vice City radio station that retained its complete original soundtrack across all versions -- no songs were removed due to copyright expiration, making it unique among the game's stations.

### Notable Tracks

The playlist includes landmark new wave tracks such as Frankie Goes to Hollywood's "Relax," Nena's "99 Luftballons," and Soft Cell's "Tainted Love."
