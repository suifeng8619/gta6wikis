## FlyLo FM

FlyLo FM is one of 26 radio stations in GTA V (2013) and GTA Online. Curated by acclaimed producer Flying Lotus, the station features experimental electronic and hip-hop fusion music.

### Station Character

FlyLo FM represents one of GTA V's innovations in radio design -- enlisting real-world music artists as curators rather than fictional DJs. Flying Lotus brings his distinctive experimental approach, blending electronic beats with hip-hop elements.
