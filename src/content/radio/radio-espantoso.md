## Radio Espantoso

Radio Espantoso is one of the nine radio stations in GTA Vice City (2002). Hosted by Pepe, the station plays Latin jazz, mambo, salsa, and Latin funk. The station features 16 tracks. The name "Espantoso" means "terrible" or "dreadful" in Spanish.

### Station Character

Radio Espantoso brings the vibrant sounds of Cuban and Latin American music to Vice City, reflecting the city's strong Latin cultural presence. The station features lively, fast-paced rhythms perfect for the tropical Vice City atmosphere.

### Featured Artists

The station showcases legendary Latin musicians including Cachao, Irakere, Deodato, Xavier Cugat and His Orchestra, Mongo Santamaria, and Beny More.
