## Wildstyle Pirate Radio

Wildstyle Pirate Radio is one of the nine radio stations in GTA Vice City (2002). Hosted by "Super Rocking" Mr. Magic (voiced by Ras Kass), the station plays pioneering 1980s hip-hop and electro music. The station features 16 tracks.

### Station Character

Wildstyle represents the early hip-hop movement of the 1980s, featuring the genre's pioneers and trailblazers. The station captures the raw energy and creativity of hip-hop's formative years.

### Notable Tracks

The playlist includes landmark hip-hop tracks like Grandmaster Flash & Furious Five's "The Message," Run-D.M.C's "Rock Box," and Afrika Bambaataa's "Looking For The Perfect Beat."
