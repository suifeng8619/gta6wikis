## Overview

The Seaplane is an amphibious float plane confirmed in GTA 6's first official trailer. Capable of landing on both water and conventional runways, it is uniquely suited for Leonida's island-dotted coastline and waterway network.

## Performance

The Seaplane offers versatile takeoff and landing options with moderate speed. It trades some aerial performance for amphibious capability.

## Acquisition

The Seaplane may be found at coastal docks, marinas, or island airstrips throughout Leonida.
