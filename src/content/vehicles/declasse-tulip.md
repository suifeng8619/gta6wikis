## Overview

The Declasse Tulip is arguably the most significant vehicle in GTA 6. Featured prominently in the first official trailer, the orange Tulip serves as the getaway car for protagonists Jason and Lucia, appearing multiple times throughout the trailer (0:30-0:50, 1:15-1:30). Based on the 1972 Chevrolet Chevelle Malibu, it embodies classic American muscle car design with a long hood, aggressive side lines, and a bold 1970s silhouette.

## Performance

The Tulip delivers typical muscle car performance: strong acceleration with heavy tire grip. It prioritizes straight-line speed over cornering agility, consistent with its real-world counterpart.

## Customization

The Tulip is confirmed to support modifications. As a key story vehicle, it likely receives extensive customization options including engine upgrades, body kits, and custom paint jobs.
