## Overview

The Buzzard Attack Chopper is a light armed helicopter returning to GTA 6. Based on the Bell AH-1Z Viper, it has been one of the most popular combat helicopters in GTA history, equipped with machine guns and rocket launchers.

## Performance

The Buzzard combines good speed and agility with combat capability. It is more maneuverable than heavy helicopters but less armored.

## Armament

The Buzzard is expected to retain its signature weapon loadout including machine guns and homing rockets.
