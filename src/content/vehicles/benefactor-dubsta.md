## Overview

The Benefactor Dubsta is a luxury off-road SUV returning to GTA 6. Based on the Mercedes-Benz G-Class, it was spotted in the first trailer in a black finish parked outside a Pawn and Gun shop. The 6x6 variant was also confirmed, featuring three axles inspired by the Mercedes-Benz G63 Brabus 6x6.

## Performance

The Dubsta provides excellent off-road capability with strong low-speed torque, though its boxy design limits aerodynamic efficiency. Specific performance values have not been confirmed.

## Customization

The Dubsta supports customization including luxury interior options, performance upgrades, and paint finishes.
