## Overview

The Declasse Vigero is a classic American muscle car returning to GTA 6, based on the Chevrolet Camaro. Confirmed in the first official trailer, it brings the iconic pony car design to the streets of Vice City.

## Performance

The Vigero delivers muscle car standard performance with strong acceleration and a heavy, less agile handling profile. Specific stats have not been confirmed for GTA 6.

## Customization

The Vigero supports full customization including engine upgrades, body modifications, wheel options, and paint finishes.
