## Overview

The Jobuilt Phantom is a heavy-duty semi-truck confirmed in GTA 6's first official trailer. Based on the Volvo FH16 and Scania heavy trucks, it is the primary long-haul cargo hauler on Leonida's highways. The cab can be detached from its trailer.

## Performance

The Phantom has slow acceleration and requires long braking distances, but delivers immense pulling power. Its large size demands careful driving, especially in urban areas.

## Acquisition

The Phantom can be found on highways or purchased for trucking missions.
