## Overview

The Swift Deluxe is a premium luxury helicopter returning to GTA 6. Based on the Sikorsky S-76, it serves as VIP transport for Leonida's wealthy elite, featuring a refined interior and smooth flight characteristics.

## Performance

The Swift Deluxe offers smooth, stable flight with moderate speed. It prioritizes passenger comfort over raw performance.
