## Overview

The Vapid Police Cruiser is the standard police patrol vehicle confirmed in GTA 6's first official trailer. Based on the Ford Police Interceptor Utility, it serves as the primary law enforcement vehicle across Leonida, equipped with sirens, lights, and high-performance pursuit capabilities.

## Performance

The Police Cruiser offers high speed, strong acceleration, and good durability for pursuit situations. It outperforms most civilian vehicles.

## Acquisition

The Police Cruiser cannot be purchased. It can be obtained by seizing one during police encounters or at specific mission locations.
