## Overview

The Albany Primo is a classic American full-size sedan confirmed in GTA 6's first official trailer. Based on the late 1980s Pontiac Bonneville, it features a rounded body design typical of that era, with both standard and Custom variants available.

## Performance

The Primo offers moderate acceleration, comfortable ride quality, and acceptable handling for city driving. Specific performance values have not been confirmed.

## Customization

The Primo supports customization. The Custom variant suggests extensive modification options including lowrider-style upgrades, custom paint, and performance tuning.
