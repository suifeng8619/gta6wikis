## Overview

The Brute Tipper is a commercial dump truck returning to GTA 6. A common sight in construction zones and industrial areas across Leonida, it serves as a heavy-duty hauler for construction materials.

## Performance

The Tipper is slow and cumbersome with a large turning radius. Its heavy-duty construction makes it durable in collisions.

## Acquisition

The Tipper can be found in construction zones and industrial areas throughout Leonida.
