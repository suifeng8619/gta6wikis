## Overview

The Ambulance is a medical emergency response vehicle returning to GTA 6. Based on a Ford Transit ambulance conversion, it provides emergency medical services across Leonida. As in previous GTA titles, it can be found near hospitals and responding to incidents.

## Performance

The Ambulance offers moderate speed and stable handling suitable for emergency response driving.

## Acquisition

The Ambulance can be found at hospitals or seized during emergency response events. It may trigger paramedic side missions when entered.
