## Overview

The Grotti Furia is a multi-generation Ferrari-inspired supercar confirmed in GTA 6's first official trailer. The front end draws from the Ferrari 488 Pista, the rear section is influenced by the SF90 Stradale, and the side profile takes cues from the Pininfarina Battista.

## Performance

As a Grotti supercar (the in-game Ferrari counterpart), the Furia is expected to deliver top-tier performance. Specific stats have not been officially confirmed.
