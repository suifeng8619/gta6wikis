## Overview

The Police Maverick is the standard police helicopter returning to GTA 6. A police-liveried variant of the civilian Maverick, it is deployed during high-wanted-level pursuits for aerial surveillance and support.

## Performance

The Police Maverick shares the civilian Maverick's flight characteristics with identical speed and handling. It is equipped with a searchlight for pursuit operations.

## Acquisition

The Police Maverick appears during high wanted level situations. It cannot be purchased but can be found at police helipads.
