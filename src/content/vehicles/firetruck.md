## Overview

The Fire Truck is a heavy-duty fire engine returning to GTA 6. Equipped with water pump systems and a ladder, it serves the fire departments of Leonida. As in previous GTA titles, it can be found at fire stations.

## Performance

The Fire Truck is slow but powerful, with strong pushing force. Its large size requires wide turns and long braking distances.

## Acquisition

The Fire Truck can be found at fire stations throughout Leonida. Entering one may trigger firefighter side missions.
