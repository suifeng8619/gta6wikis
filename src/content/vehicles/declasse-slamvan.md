## Overview

The Declasse Slamvan is a lowered custom van confirmed in GTA 6's first official trailer. Featuring dropped suspension, oversized wheels, and custom bodywork, the Slamvan represents the lowrider van culture.

## Performance

The Slamvan offers basic van performance with limited speed and acceleration. Its lowered stance and custom suspension prioritize style over utility.

## Customization

The Slamvan is confirmed as a heavily modified vehicle, supporting extensive customization including paint jobs, wheel upgrades, and body modifications.
