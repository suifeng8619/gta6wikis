## Overview

The Western Sovereign is a classic American cruiser motorcycle confirmed in GTA 6's first official trailer. Based on Harley-Davidson cruiser models, it delivers the iconic low-slung, chrome-heavy aesthetic that defines American motorcycle culture.

## Performance

The Sovereign offers strong low-end torque and comfortable highway cruising. It trades nimble handling for stability and presence. Specific performance values have not been confirmed.

## Customization

The Sovereign supports extensive customization including chrome accessories, exhaust pipes, saddlebags, and custom paint finishes.
