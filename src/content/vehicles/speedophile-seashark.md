## Overview

The Speedophile Seashark is a personal watercraft confirmed in GTA 6's second official trailer. Based on the Sea-Doo RXT, it returns to the GTA series with improved visual design and enhanced water physics. Featured in the trailer's opening sequence, it showcases GTA 6's advanced water simulation.

## Performance

The Seashark offers high-speed straight-line performance and responsive handling on water. Specific performance values have not been confirmed.
