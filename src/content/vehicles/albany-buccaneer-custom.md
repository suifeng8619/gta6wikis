## Overview

The Albany Buccaneer Custom is a classic lowrider muscle car confirmed in GTA 6's first official trailer. Based on the Buick Riviera, the Custom variant features enhanced customization typical of lowrider culture, fitting perfectly with Vice City's vibrant car scene.

## Performance

As a classic muscle car, the Buccaneer Custom prioritizes style over raw speed. It offers moderate performance suitable for cruising. Specific stats have not been confirmed.

## Customization

The Custom variant indicates extensive modification options including hydraulics, custom paint, wire wheels, and interior upgrades.
