## Overview

The Bravado Buffalo Police is a high-performance police patrol vehicle confirmed in GTA 6's first trailer. Based on the Dodge Charger Pursuit, it provides law enforcement with muscle-car-level performance for high-speed pursuits across Leonida.

## Performance

The Buffalo Police offers exceptional acceleration and top speed for a patrol car, making it one of the more formidable police vehicles players will encounter.

## Acquisition

The Buffalo Police cannot be purchased. It can be seized during police encounters or found at police stations.
