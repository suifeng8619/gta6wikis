## Overview

The Maibatsu Benson is a medium-duty commercial box truck returning to GTA 6. A common sight on Leonida's streets, it serves as a delivery and cargo transport vehicle.

## Performance

The Benson offers moderate truck performance with decent cargo capacity and manageable handling for urban deliveries.

## Acquisition

The Benson can be found throughout Leonida's commercial areas and may be used in delivery-related missions.
