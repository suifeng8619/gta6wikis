The Maibatsu Penumbra is a lightweight compact sports car confirmed in GTA 6's second official trailer. Its two-door design blends elements of the Mitsubishi Eclipse GT and Audi TT, offering a nimble and affordable sports car option.

The Penumbra emphasizes agile handling and quick acceleration thanks to its lightweight construction. Specific performance values have not been confirmed.
