## Overview

The Airboat is a flat-bottomed swamp vessel confirmed in GTA 6's first official trailer. A quintessential Florida vehicle, it uses a rear-mounted propeller to glide across shallow swamp waters. Perfectly suited for Leonida's Everglades-inspired wetlands, it enables exploration of areas inaccessible to conventional boats.

## Performance

The Airboat excels in shallow water navigation with high passability across swamp terrain. It trades speed for versatility in navigating difficult waterways.

## Acquisition

The Airboat may be available as a mission-specific vehicle or found parked in swamp regions throughout Leonida.
