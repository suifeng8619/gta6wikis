## Overview

The Vapid Chino is a classic lowrider sedan confirmed in GTA 6's first official trailer. Based on the Chevrolet Impala, it features lowered suspension and a classic American cruiser aesthetic that fits perfectly with Vice City's car culture.

## Performance

The Chino offers moderate performance suited for cruising rather than racing. Specific performance values have not been confirmed.

## Customization

The Chino is designed for customization, supporting hydraulics, custom paint, wire wheels, and lowrider-style modifications.
