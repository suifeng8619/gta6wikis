The Pfister Astron is a sporty compact SUV confirmed in GTA 6's first official trailer. Based on the Porsche Macan, it blends Porsche's performance DNA with the practicality of an SUV form factor.

## Performance

The Astron is expected to be one of the more agile SUVs in GTA 6, with handling characteristics that lean toward sports cars. Specific performance values have not been confirmed.
