## Overview

The Mammoth Dodo is a small single-engine light aircraft returning to GTA 6. Based on the Cessna 172, this iconic GTA series aircraft was spotted in official screenshots flying over Leonida's islands. It retains its classic design but receives a visual quality upgrade for the new generation.

## Performance

The Dodo offers slow cruising speed, easy handling, and low fuel consumption, making it the most beginner-friendly aircraft in the game.

## Acquisition

The Dodo may be available at airports or through missions. It has historically been one of the most accessible aircraft in the GTA series.
