## Overview

The Pegassi Zorrusso is a wide-body two-door supercar confirmed in GTA 6's first official trailer. It features an aggressive front fascia inspired by the Italdesign Zerouno, tail lights reminiscent of the Bugatti Divo, and side vents drawing from the Lamborghini Aventador SV. The rear spoiler echoes the Aventador SVJ.

## Performance

As a top-tier hypercar manufactured by Pegassi (the in-game counterpart of Lamborghini), the Zorrusso is expected to deliver exceptional straight-line speed and acceleration. Specific performance values have not been officially confirmed.

## Acquisition

The Zorrusso's acquisition method has not been confirmed. Based on its class, it will likely be available for purchase at a premium price or unlocked through story missions.

## Customization

The Zorrusso is confirmed to support customization, likely including body kits, paint jobs, wheel upgrades, and performance tuning at Ride Out Customs.
