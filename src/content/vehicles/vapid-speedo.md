## Overview

The Vapid Speedo is a light commercial delivery van confirmed in GTA 6's first official trailer, seen on the highway. Based on the Ford Transit, it serves as a versatile workhorse for deliveries, small business operations, and various missions.

## Performance

The Speedo offers moderate speed, strong cargo capacity, and nimble steering for a van. Specific performance values have not been confirmed.
