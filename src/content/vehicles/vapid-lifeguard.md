## Overview

The Vapid Lifeguard is a beach rescue vehicle confirmed in GTA 6's first official trailer. Seen patrolling Vice Beach, it adds to the authentic Florida-inspired atmosphere of Leonida's coastline. Based on a Ford truck platform, it is painted in classic lifeguard colors.

## Performance

The Lifeguard truck offers basic truck performance suitable for beach patrol operations. It is not designed for high-speed pursuits.

## Acquisition

The Lifeguard truck may be found parked along Vice Beach or obtained during beach-related missions.
