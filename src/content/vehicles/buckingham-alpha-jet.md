## Overview

The Buckingham Alpha is a private business jet returning to GTA 6. Based on the Bombardier Learjet, it provides fast long-range air travel for wealthy players who need to traverse Leonida quickly.

## Performance

The Alpha offers high cruising speed and long range, making it ideal for cross-map travel. It requires a runway for takeoff and landing.

## Acquisition

The Alpha may be available for purchase at high cost or found at private airfield terminals.
