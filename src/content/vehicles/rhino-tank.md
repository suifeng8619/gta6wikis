## Overview

The Rhino Tank is the iconic main battle tank expected to return in GTA 6. A series staple since GTA III, the Rhino provides unmatched firepower and armor protection, typically available only through military encounters or high wanted levels.

## Performance

The Rhino is extremely slow but virtually indestructible against small arms fire. Its main cannon delivers devastating explosive damage.

## Acquisition

The Rhino Tank is typically obtained at maximum wanted levels or through specific military-themed missions. It cannot be purchased from standard dealerships.
