The Nagasaki Carbon RS is a high-performance superbike confirmed in GTA 6's first official trailer. Inspired by the Ducati Panigale and Kawasaki Ninja, it focuses on extreme speed, minimal weight, and sharp handling.

## Performance

As a top-tier superbike, the Carbon RS is expected to deliver exceptional speed and acceleration with razor-sharp handling. Specific performance values have not been confirmed.
