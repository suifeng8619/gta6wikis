## Overview

The Maibatsu Sanchez is a lightweight dirt bike confirmed in GTA 6's first official trailer. Based on the Honda XR/CRF series, it is purpose-built for off-road riding across Leonida's swamps, dirt trails, and rugged terrain.

## Performance

The Sanchez excels in off-road conditions with high ground clearance, light weight, and nimble handling. It is less suited for high-speed highway riding. Specific performance values have not been confirmed.

## Customization

The Sanchez supports customization including exhaust modifications, graphics kits, and suspension upgrades.
