## Overview

The News Helicopter is a media broadcast helicopter confirmed in GTA 6's first official trailer. It provides aerial news coverage of events across Vice City, adding to the living world atmosphere. Players may encounter it during high-profile events or police chases.

## Performance

The News Helicopter offers standard civilian helicopter performance with a focus on stable hovering for camera operations.

## Acquisition

The News Helicopter may be found at media company helipads or encountered during major in-game events.
