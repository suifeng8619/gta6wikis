## Overview

The Brute Police Riot is a heavy armored law enforcement vehicle confirmed in GTA 6's first official trailer. Based on the Lenco BEAR armored assault vehicle, it is deployed during high-danger situations and can withstand significant damage while pushing through barriers.

## Performance

The Police Riot is slow due to its heavy armor but offers extreme durability and the ability to ram through obstacles and barricades.

## Acquisition

The Police Riot cannot be purchased. It appears during high wanted level encounters and specific mission events.
