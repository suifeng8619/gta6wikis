## Overview

The Vapid Caracara 4x4 is a high-performance off-road pickup truck confirmed in GTA 6's first trailer. Based on the Ford F-150 Raptor and Ford Bronco, it appears in orange as a lifeguard vehicle on Vice Beach and was also featured in the Thrillbilly mud club scene. It was additionally seen as Brian Heder's vehicle in Trailer 2.

## Performance

The Caracara 4x4 offers strong acceleration, excellent off-road capability, and responsive steering for its size. Specific performance values have not been confirmed.

## Customization

The Caracara 4x4 supports full customization including lift kits, wider tires, body armor, and performance tuning.
