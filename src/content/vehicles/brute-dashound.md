## Overview

The Brute Dashound is a long-distance coach bus confirmed in GTA 6's first official trailer, visible during the opening highway sequence (0:00-0:10). Based on the MCI Renaissance E series (the in-game version of Greyhound), it provides inter-city passenger transport across Leonida.

## Performance

The Dashound is slow with a long body that requires wide turning radius. It carries large numbers of passengers but is not suited for quick maneuvers.

## Acquisition

The Dashound is primarily an NPC-operated vehicle. It may be available in specific missions or stolen from bus stations.
