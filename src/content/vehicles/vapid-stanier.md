## Overview

The Vapid Stanier is a full-size American sedan returning to GTA 6. Based on the Ford Crown Victoria, it has historically served as one of the most common civilian vehicles in the GTA series, often seen as taxi cabs and unmarked police cars.

## Performance

The Stanier provides reliable sedan performance with moderate speed and handling. It is a workhorse rather than a performance car. Specific stats have not been confirmed for GTA 6.

## Customization

The Stanier supports basic customization including paint finishes and wheel options.
