## Overview

The Declasse Granger 3600LX is a massive full-size American SUV confirmed in GTA 6's first trailer at the 0:27 mark. Based on the Chevrolet Suburban and Tahoe, it features a boxy square design and imposing presence typical of large American SUVs.

## Performance

The Granger 3600LX offers strong towing capability and low-speed torque, though its large size makes it less agile in tight spaces. Specific performance values have not been confirmed.

## Customization

The Granger 3600LX supports customization including suspension modifications, paint finishes, and wheel upgrades.
