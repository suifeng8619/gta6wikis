## Overview

The Buckingham Maverick is a versatile civilian helicopter returning to GTA 6. Based on the Bell 206L LongRanger and Robinson R66, it has been a staple of the GTA series for transportation, observation, and aerial operations. Confirmed through official screenshots.

## Performance

The Maverick offers a stable flight platform with moderate speed and good durability. It is the most approachable helicopter for new pilots.

## Customization

The Maverick supports customization including paint finishes and limited upgrades.
