## Overview

The Bravado Gauntlet Hellfire is a high-performance variant of the Gauntlet muscle car, based on the Dodge Challenger SRT Hellcat. Confirmed in GTA 6's first official trailer, it brings raw American muscle power to Vice City's streets.

## Performance

As a Hellfire variant, this Gauntlet delivers top-tier muscle car acceleration and straight-line speed, though handling remains heavy and less responsive in tight corners.

## Customization

The Gauntlet Hellfire supports full customization including engine upgrades, body modifications, custom wheels, and paint finishes.
