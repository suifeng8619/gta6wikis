## Overview

The Brute Bus is the standard city public transit bus seen in GTA 6's second trailer against the Vice City backdrop. Based on the New Flyer Xcelsior, it operates regular routes throughout the city, contributing to the living world atmosphere.

## Performance

The Bus is slow with gradual acceleration and a large turning radius. It is not designed for quick movement but serves its purpose as public transportation.

## Acquisition

The Bus is primarily NPC-operated. Players may board it as a passenger or commandeer one for missions.
