## Overview

The Vapid Sandking XL is a massive lifted off-road truck confirmed in GTA 6's first official trailer. Based on the third-generation Ford Super Duty, it towers over other vehicles and is built for extreme off-road conditions across Leonida's varied terrain.

## Performance

The Sandking XL offers exceptional off-road capability with high ground clearance and powerful torque. Its large size makes it slower and less maneuverable on paved roads. Specific performance values have not been confirmed.

## Customization

The Sandking XL supports customization including lift kits, oversized tires, bull bars, and performance modifications.
