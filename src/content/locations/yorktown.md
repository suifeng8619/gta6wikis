## Overview

Yorktown is a small inland town in western Kelly County. According to leaked information, the town is located northbound on Route 2, near Bocamar Bridge and the Gas Stop toward Port Gellhorn. It represents Leonida's rural and agricultural community character — quiet, simple, and far removed from Vice City's chaos.

## Town Character

Yorktown embodies the typical American small town:
- Peaceful and unpretentious atmosphere
- Local community feel
- Agricultural surroundings
- A hub connecting urban areas to wilderness
