## Overview

Kelly County is a major administrative division in southwestern Leonida. The county encompasses multiple important cities and regions including Port Gellhorn (its main city) and connections to Yorktown and other settlements. The county is composed of coastal port areas, agricultural fields, and swamp wilderness — representing the diverse geography beyond Vice City's urban core.

## Major Areas

### Port Gellhorn
- Declining western coastal port city
- Cheap motels and closed attractions
- Port industrial facilities
- Worker-class communities

### Yorktown
- Inland town in western Kelly County
- Near Bocamar Bridge
- Along Route 2

### Grassrivers (Partial)
- Eastern swamp and wetlands
- Dangerous wildlife
- Natural wilderness
