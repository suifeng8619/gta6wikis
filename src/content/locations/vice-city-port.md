## Overview

Vice City Port (also known as Viceport or VC Port) is Vice City's main maritime transportation hub, located along the city's coastline. The port connects Vice City to surrounding waters and islands, serving as a major facility for both cruise ships and cargo operations. It is described as the "cruise capital," reflecting Miami's real-world status as a leading cruise port.

## Port Facilities

### Maritime Infrastructure
- **Container yards** - Large areas for stacked cargo containers
- **Port warehouses** - Cargo storage buildings
- **Docks and berths** - Ship mooring areas
- **Loading equipment** - Cranes and cargo handling machinery

### Key Areas
- **Cruise ship terminal** - Large cruise liner docking area
- **Cargo operations zone** - Commercial ship handling area
- **Port offices** - Administrative facilities
- **Customs facilities** - Cargo inspection areas

## Activities

- **Smuggling missions** - Illegal cargo operations (main/side missions)
- **Ship-related tasks** - Water transportation activities
- **Cargo operations** - Port work-related missions
- **Maritime navigation** - Ship handling
- **Exploration** - Port area discovery

## Commercial Functions

The port serves multiple commercial purposes:
- **Cruise capital** - Major cruise ship departure point
- **Cargo center** - Commercial goods transportation
- **Smuggling hub** - Illegal cargo operations (game narrative)

## Neighborhood Connections

- East of Vice City center
- South of Ocean Beach
- Close to Downtown Vice City
- Connected to Leonida Keys
