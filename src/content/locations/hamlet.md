## Overview

Hamlet is a confirmed location within Leonida, appearing three times in the leaked debug menu as different vehicle racing positions. The name and its "Homestead" annotation suggest an agricultural community settlement. It represents one of Leonida's most remote and rural areas, far from any city center.

## Racing Activities

Hamlet's primary confirmed gameplay function is vehicle racing:
- **3 different race courses** confirmed through leaked debug menu
- Racing appears to be a major activity in the area
- The open rural roads and farmland provide ideal racing terrain

## Key Features

- **Racing course locations** - Multiple confirmed race tracks
- **Agricultural buildings** - Farms and farm-related structures
- **Open countryside** - Expansive agricultural fields and open spaces
- **Rural road network** - Country roads connecting the community
- **Scattered homesteads** - Dispersed family residences

Typical rural agricultural community style:
- **Simple farmhouses** - Farmer residences

Hamlet has not appeared in official trailers. It was first confirmed through the leaked debug menu, where it appears as a vehicle racing activity location with three separate race courses.
