## Overview

Grassrivers is an expansive swamp and wetland region in southern Leonida, spanning the boundary between Vice-Dale County and Kelly County. The name likely references Marjory Stoneman Douglas's book "The Everglades: River of Grass" (1947). It is described as the "mysterious heart" of Leonida — a wild and beautiful swamp filled with danger and secrets.

## Wildlife

### Confirmed Animals
- **Alligators** - The apex predator of the swamp
- **Snakes** - Various water and land species
- **Frogs** - Swamp amphibians
- **Wading birds** - Herons, grebes, and other species
- **Deer** - Land mammals
- **Wild boar** - Found at forest and swamp edges
- **Florida panther** - Possible apex predator

## Advanced Environmental Technology

Grassrivers showcases GTA 6's technical achievements:
- **Particle effects** - Helicopter downwash creates water ripples, blows vegetation, and kicks up dust (unprecedented in GTA games)
- **Smart NPC behavior** - Police described as "smarter, faster, and more dangerous" in complex swamp terrain
- **Environmental interaction** - Real-time physics between helicopters and the swamp environment
- **Dense vegetation** - "Everything from reeds to trees looks real" (official description)

## Key Features

- **Vast grass wetlands** - Massive swampland areas
- **Reed fields** - Dense aquatic vegetation
- **Mud flats** - Soft terrain areas
- **Waterways** - Swamp water network
- **Tree islands** - Dry land patches within the swamp
- **Hidden camps** - Secret stopping points
