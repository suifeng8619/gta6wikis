## Overview

Ambrosia is a rough, industrialized inland area in central Leonida, situated on the southwestern shore of Lake Leonida. Surrounded by vast swamplands and agricultural fields, it serves as Leonida's agricultural and industrial center. This is not a tourist destination — it is a place of work and survival.

## Allied Crystal Sugar Refinery

The Allied Crystal sugar refinery is the economic centerpiece of Ambrosia and its largest employer. Inspired by real sugar corporations like the United States Sugar Corporation based in Clewiston, Florida, the facility is a massive industrial complex that dominates the city.

Official descriptions mention "intense gunfights" taking place within the factory buildings, making it a significant mission location.
