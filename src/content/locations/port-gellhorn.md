## Overview

Port Gellhorn is the main city of Kelly County, located on Leonida's western coast. It is described as "Leonida's forgotten coast" — a declining industrial port city filled with cheap roadside motels, closed attractions, and empty shopping malls. The entire city is sustained by "malt liquor, painkillers, and truck stop energy drinks," presenting a stark contrast to Vice City's glamour.

## City Character

Port Gellhorn represents the dark side of the American dream — a once-functional port city now in economic decline. The atmosphere is one of desperation and decay, with abandoned businesses, struggling residents, and a general sense of hopelessness. It serves as GTA 6's commentary on industrial decline in America.
