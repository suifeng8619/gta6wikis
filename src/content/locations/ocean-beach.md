## Overview

Ocean Beach is the southern end of Vice Beach, located south of Route 10. It is one of Vice City's most famous neighborhoods, serving as the center for sightseeing, entertainment, and beach activities. The area embodies traditional Miami beach culture with its wide sandy beaches, palm trees, Art Deco buildings, and bustling crowds.

## Key Landmarks

### Hotel Dixon
Hotel Dixon is located on Ocean Drive and is based on the real Hotel Victor at 1144 Ocean Drive, South Beach. It features a distinctive vertical neon sign that has become one of the most recognizable landmarks in Vice City.

### Boardwalk Hotel
The Boardwalk Hotel is inspired by the famous Colony Hotel, which opened in 1935. It represents the Art Deco heritage of the area.

### Ocean Drive
The main thoroughfare is lined with hotels, restaurants, and entertainment venues. It serves as the backbone of the Ocean Beach district.

## Architecture

The district is defined by its Art Deco architectural style. Buildings are typically 3-7 stories tall, painted in vibrant colors (pink, green, blue), and decorated with curves and ornamental details. The style dates from the 1930s Art Deco era and has been faithfully recreated in GTA 6.
