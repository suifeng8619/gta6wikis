## Overview

Vice City International Airport is the main international airport serving Vice City and the surrounding Leonida region. Located near the city, close to highways and hotel districts, it serves as the primary air transportation hub for entering and exiting Vice City. The airport may also be referred to as Escobar International Airport, as mentioned in official trailer references.

## Airport Facilities

### Main Infrastructure
- **Terminal buildings** - Passenger terminals with check-in and boarding areas
- **Radar and control tower** - Air traffic control facilities
- **Tarmac and apron** - Aircraft parking and maintenance areas
- **Runways** - Aircraft takeoff and landing strips

### Terminal Interior
The terminal is confirmed to be enterable, featuring:
- Security screening areas
- Baggage handling zones
- Check-in counters
- Departure lounges
- Restaurants and retail shops
- Restroom facilities

### Surrounding Facilities
- **Parking structures** - Vehicle parking areas
- **Airport hotels** - Nearby accommodation
- **Taxi and transit hub** - Ground transportation
- **Automated people mover** - Internal transport system (officially mentioned)

## Gameplay Functions

The airport serves multiple gameplay purposes:
- **Transportation hub** - Connecting Vice City with other Leonida regions
- **Story driver** - Supporting main and side missions
- **Open world exploration** - Providing a large enterable facility

## Neighborhood Connections

- Near Vice City's southern boundary
- Close to Highway 97
- Connected to Vice City's main transportation network
- Near Kelly County

## Trailer Appearances

The airport appears in official GTA 6 trailers showing:
- **"Vice City" signage** on the airport
- **Radar and control tower** visible in the background
- **Aircraft** parked and taking off
- **Surrounding hotel and support facilities**
