## Overview

Stockyard is located in the northern part of Vice City near the coastline. It is Vice City's industrial and creative hub, famous for its warehouse conversions and extensive street art. The district represents the artistic renaissance of a former industrial area, inspired by Miami's Wynwood neighborhood.

## Street Art and Murals

Street art is the defining feature of Stockyard. Nearly every wall surface is covered with colorful graffiti and murals. The large-scale works transform the industrial landscape into an open-air art gallery, directly referencing Miami's famous Wynwood Walls project.

## Key Landmarks

- **Large-scale graffiti walls** - Eye-catching street art covering building facades
- **Industrial warehouses** - Former warehouses converted for commercial and creative use
- **Street art corridors** - Multiple streets decorated with murals
- **Event spaces** - Open areas for car meets and gatherings

## Activities

- **Car meets** - Automobile gatherings confirmed in official trailers
- **Street racing** - Racing through warehouse district streets
- **Art exploration** - Discovering murals and street art
- **Shopping** - Small stores (according to leaked info, all small shops are enterable)

## Architecture

The district features converted industrial buildings — large concrete and brick warehouse structures repurposed for retail, dining, and creative spaces. The industrial heritage remains visible in the raw materials and large-scale structures, while the street art provides a vibrant counterpoint.

## Neighborhood Connections

- Close to Downtown Vice City
- Vice Beach to the south
- Connected to Vice City's main transportation network

## Trailer Appearances

Stockyard appears prominently in official GTA 6 trailers, showing car meet scenes with large crowds of vehicles and people gathered on streets covered in graffiti art. The vibrant, energetic atmosphere of the district is clearly visible.
