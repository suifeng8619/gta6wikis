## Overview

Leonida Keys is a chain of tropical islands south of Vice City, connected to Vice-Dale County via the Keys Causeway through Mariana County. This archipelago represents a tropical paradise escape, described as having a "laid-back but not flashy" lifestyle — a stark contrast to Vice City's urban energy. However, the beautiful surrounding waters are noted to be filled with danger.

## Story Significance

Leonida Keys plays a crucial role in GTA 6's narrative:
- **Jason Duval and Lucia Caminos** reside here after their release from prison
- **Jason's house** is provided free of charge by Brian Heder
- The Keys serve as a safe haven and potential story development location
- Represents the transition from urban life to secluded retreat

## Marine Life and Environment

Official resources describe rich marine biodiversity:
- **Eels** - Common species in seagrass beds
- **Sea turtles** - Endangered species (protected in real Florida Keys)
- **Coral** - Forming reef ecosystems
- **Tropical fish** - Various species
- **Dangerous marine creatures** - The game describes "beautiful but dangerous waters"

## Key Landmarks

- **Jason Duval's house** - Main character's residence, lent by Brian Heder
- **Diving sites** - Multiple underwater exploration points
- **Keys Causeway** - The bridge connecting the Keys to the mainland
- **Coral reefs** - Underwater ecosystems
- **White sand beaches** - Pristine tropical shorelines
- **Resort town** - Shopping and service facilities

## Architecture

Buildings reflect the tropical resort environment with light colors (white, pale blue, brown) and open designs. Common elements include outdoor areas and terraces, ventilated designs for tropical climate, wooden decks and seaside walkways, and modern vacation villa styling.

## Neighborhood Connections

- South of Vice City
- West of Grassrivers
- Connected to Vice-Dale County via Keys Causeway
- Geographically the southernmost Leonida region
