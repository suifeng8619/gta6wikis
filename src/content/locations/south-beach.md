## Overview

South Beach, also known as Vice Beach, is the most vibrant and modern beach area in Vice City. Comprising North Beach, Mid Beach, and South Beach, this district is defined by wide sandy beaches, luxury hotels, high-end dining, and a thriving nightlife scene. It is the beating heart of Vice City's tourism and entertainment.

## Key Landmarks

### Bayfront Heights
Located on the northern side of Vice Beach, Bayfront Heights is dominated by a building inspired by the Royal Palm South Beach Miami resort. This area represents the peak of luxury in the beach district.

### Hotels and Resorts
- **Royal Palm South Beach-inspired building** - The centerpiece of Bayfront Heights
- **Loews Hotels** - Chain hotel presence in the area
- **Multiple five-star resort hotels** - Upscale accommodation facilities

## Activities

Official trailers have confirmed several activities in the area:
- **Beach parties** - Large gatherings on the sand shown in trailers
- **Roller skating** - Skaters gliding along the beachfront
- **Shopping** - High-end retail along the boardwalk
- **Dining and bars** - Upscale restaurants and nightlife venues
- **Water sports** - Various beach-related activities

## Culture

South Beach represents Miami's international character, fashion, and modernity. The area reflects strong South American and Caribbean cultural influences, mirroring Miami's status as an international trade and tourism hub.

## Neighborhood Connections

- Ocean Beach to the south
- Downtown Vice City to the north
- Close to Vice City Port
- Connected to the broader Vice Beach area
