## GTA Online

GTA Online is the multiplayer component of Grand Theft Auto V, launched October 1, 2013. It evolved from a multiplayer mode into a standalone cultural phenomenon and revenue powerhouse. By 2025, GTA Online had generated over $8 billion in revenue, making it the most profitable multiplayer game ever created.

### Evolution

GTA Online launched with basic free roam, deathmatch, and capture modes, then expanded through 100+ significant updates:

- **2015**: Heists Update introduced 4-player cooperative heist missions
- **2015-2017**: CEO/VIP System, Bikers Update, Gunrunning
- **2019**: Diamond Casino Heist with $6-11.484M payouts and three approach options
- **2020**: Cayo Perico Heist -- the first solo-capable heist with $4.5M+ payouts
- **2023**: The Agency with Dr. Dre story missions
- **2024**: Bottom Dollar Bounties bail enforcement system

### Business Empire

Players can build criminal enterprises through multiple income-generating properties:

- **Nightclub** - $50K-$150K daily income
- **Cocaine Lockup** - $30K-$100K per batch
- **Acid Lab** - $500K-$1.2M per batch
- **Auto Shop** - Vehicle robbery and customization
- **Arcade** - Passive income and Diamond Casino Heist access

### Monetization

Primary revenue comes from Shark Card sales ($20-$100 real money options). Annual revenue peaked at $500M-$1B+ per year. The game maintains millions of daily active players.

### Heists

The Cayo Perico Heist (2020) is the most efficient money-making method, requiring a $2.2M submarine investment with 85%+ return on each run. The Diamond Casino Heist offers three distinct approaches: Silent & Sneaky, Big Con, and Aggressive.

### Cultural Impact

GTA Online set the standard for live-service game models, influenced industry monetization approaches, and created a major streaming and content creation platform. Heavy roleplay communities thrive on FiveM servers.
