## Grand Theft Auto V

Grand Theft Auto V is the most commercially successful main entry in the GTA series, released in September 2013. The game broke entertainment industry records and became a cultural phenomenon, with re-releases on PlayStation 4, Xbox One, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X/S maintaining relevance over a decade after launch.

### Three Protagonists

GTA V revolutionized gameplay with three simultaneously playable protagonists:

- **Michael De Santa** (voiced by Ned Luke) - Retired bank robber in witness protection, mid-40s, pulled back into crime
- **Franklin Clinton** (voiced by Shawn Fonteno) - Young street hustler in his early 20s, ambitious and intelligent
- **Trevor Philips** (voiced by Steven Ogg) - Unhinged meth cook and former military pilot, charismatic yet terrifying

Each character has a unique special ability: Michael's Bullet Time, Franklin's vehicle slow-motion, and Trevor's damage reduction rampage mode.

### Heist System

GTA V introduced sophisticated heist mechanics with planning phases, multiple approach options, preparation missions, and crew management. Major heists include The Jewel Store Job, The Pacific Standard Job, and The Big Score (with payouts up to $11.484 million).

### Los Santos

The map spans approximately 127 square kilometers (49 square miles), including urban Los Santos, suburban sprawl, and rural Blaine County wilderness. Key districts include Vinewood, Downtown, Sandy Shores, Paleto Bay, and Rockford Hills.

### Critical Reception

Metacritic scores of 97/100 across PlayStation 3, Xbox 360, PlayStation 4, and PC. Praised for revolutionary open-world design, character writing, heist mechanics, and technical achievement.

### Sales Records

- 11.21 million copies sold in 24 hours (fastest-selling entertainment product at the time)
- Over 220 million copies total as of 2025
- Third best-selling video game of all time (behind Tetris and Minecraft)
- First entertainment product to generate $1 billion in sales
- Over $8 billion total revenue including GTA Online

### Radio Stations

GTA V features 26 radio stations with 750+ songs, including stations curated by real artists like Frank Ocean (blonded Los Santos) and Flying Lotus (FlyLo FM).
